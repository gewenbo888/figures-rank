# 100 Most Influential Figures in History · Ranked | 人类历史100位最具影响力人物排行榜

A bilingual ranking of the 100 most influential people in human history, scored on Influence, Legacy, and Civilizational Reach — from ancient sages to modern revolutionaries.

## Links

- **Live:** [figures.psyverse.fun](https://figures.psyverse.fun)
- **GitHub:** [github.com/gewenbo888/figures-rank](https://github.com/gewenbo888/figures-rank)

## Coverage

- **100 figures** spanning 3 eras: Ancient & Classical, Medieval & Early Modern, Modern Era
- Scored on Influence (0–100), Legacy (0–100), and Reach (0–100)
- Sortable, filterable by era, searchable in English and Chinese
- Second tab: Era Rankings with group statistics

## Disclaimer

Scores are subjective composites drawing on historical scholarship, citation frequency, cultural longevity, and cross-civilizational reach. Rankings reflect historical impact, not moral endorsement.
